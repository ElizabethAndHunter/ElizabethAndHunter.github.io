Pittsburgh International Airport

The Pittsburgh International Airport sits approximately 20-30 minutes from downtown and is newly renovated.

While you can Uber or Lyft from the airport to your destination, Pittsburgh Regional Transit (PRT) provides bus rides from the airport to Downtown on the 28X. One way fairs start at $2.75. More information about the 28X's schedule can be found here.
